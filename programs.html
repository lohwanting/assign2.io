    Strength and Conditioning

    You’ll build functional strength for your whole body, as well as enhance your muscular endurance and boost your metabolism. Walk away feeling relaxed and with more lean muscle mass.

    Cardio

    During the workout, you’ll get your heart pumping, and increase your cardio fitness. You’ll shape and tone your butt and legs, and improving your coordination and agility. Best of all, you’ll keep burning calories long after the workout is over.

    Mind and Body

    Find your centre in this yoga-based workout that’ll improve your mind, body and life. This class uses a series a simple yoga moves and embraces elements of Tai Chi and Pilates. You will bend and stretch to an inspiring soundtrack filled with songs that will help you relax and calm yourself.

    Dance

    Welcome to the ultimate combination of dance and music. It’s all about moving through an addictive fusion of the latest dance moves choreographed to the hottest new music. And it doesn’t matter if you’re a dance master or if you have two left feet, there’s something in this class for you.

    Cycling

    This challenging indoor cycling class combines aerobic and anaerobic training. In between short, intense sprints, you’ll keep cycling to raise your heart rate. You’ll improve your cardiovascular and muscular endurance and walk away on stronger legs.

    HIIT

    This class is perfect if you want to work on multiple areas. You’ll do circuit style training that focuses on cardio, strength, mobility, agility, and reaction time. You’ll leave class with a sense of accomplishment and better overall training abilities.
