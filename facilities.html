    • OUR FACILITIES AND AMENITIES

    Our first-class facilities help you get the most out of your workout
    and gym experience.



    Swimming Pool

    Build and tone your muscles, increase your endurance and improve your cardio. Take part in Group Exercise classes like AQUAFIT and Sports Performance Swim, or simply to sunbathe and relax.

    Steam Room

    Relax after your workout in one of our steam rooms. The wet heat will boost your immune system, loosen stiff joints, improve your circulation, burn calories and lower your blood pressure.

    Cardio Room

    If you are looking for a way to take your cardio workout to the next level, the Cardio Wave machine is an innovative machine that can help you burn some calories and tone your muscles.

    Boxing Area

    We aim to provide simulation of a real boxing environment that helps you take your practice to the next level, using our high quality gym equipments.

    Friendly Front Desk

    We aim to serve you with our utmost best staff and render help with the best service so that you will enjoy your time here and have a pleasant experience with us.

    Locker

    We aim to bring added convenience and safety to you by providing you with lockers to deposit youe belongings so that you can exercise with peace of mind.
